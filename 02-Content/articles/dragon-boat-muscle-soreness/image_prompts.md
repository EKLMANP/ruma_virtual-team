# 龍舟肌肉痠痛指南 - 配圖 Prompt

本文件提供文章各段落對應的 Gemini 3 Pro 圖片生成 Prompt。

**設計風格：現代美國運動雜誌風格 (Modern American Sports Magazine Style)**
- 高對比度、動態張力
- 戲劇性光影效果
- 運動員英雄式構圖
- 乾淨俐落的視覺呈現
- 類似 ESPN、Sports Illustrated、Nike 廣告風格

---

## 封面/首圖 (Hero Image)

**Prompt for Gemini 3 Pro:**
```
Modern American sports magazine editorial photo of a muscular dragon boat paddler 
mid-stroke, back view showing defined latissimus dorsi and shoulder muscles 
engaging, high contrast dramatic lighting, sweat glistening on skin, 
water splashing dynamically, intense athletic focus, dark moody background 
to highlight the athlete, text space on top, 16:9 aspect ratio, 
ESPN Body Issue style photography
```

**用途說明：** 文章封面，強調「肌群運用」的主題。

---

## 段落一：天使痠痛 (正確肌群示意)

**Prompt for Gemini 3 Pro:**
```
Stylized anatomical sports illustration overlay on a photo of a dragon boat paddler, 
highlighting the Lats (back), Obliques (side core), and Quads (legs) 
in glowing neon orange, rest of the image in desaturated blue-grey 
to create contrast, modern infographic style, clean lines, professional 
medical-sports crossover aesthetic, 16:9 aspect ratio
```

**用途說明：** 放置於「天使痠痛」段落，視覺化正確發力的部位。

---

## 段落二：核心轉體 (Rotation)

**Prompt for Gemini 3 Pro:**
```
Low-angle action shot of a paddler fully rotated at the catch phase, 
camera looking up from water level, capturing the extreme torque of the torso, 
dramatic studio lighting with rim light highlighting the core muscles, 
water droplets frozen in mid-air, Nike commercial aesthetic, 
powerful and heroic composition, 16:9 aspect ratio
```

**用途說明：** 雖然文章中沒有獨立段落，但適合放在「核心肌群」或「正確姿勢」相關描述旁。

---

## 段落三：魔鬼痠痛 (錯誤姿勢 - 聳肩/雞翅膀)

**Prompt for Gemini 3 Pro:**
```
Editorial photography comparing "Good Form" vs "Bad Form" split screen, 
cinematic lighting. Left side (Good): Relaxed shoulders, long reach. 
Right side (Bad): Hunched shoulders, tense neck, bent "chicken wing" arm. 
Red graphical "X" element overlay on the bad form, Green check on good form, 
high-end sports magazine instructional style, 16:9 aspect ratio
```

**用途說明：** 放置於「魔鬼痠痛」段落，對比錯誤姿勢。

---

## 段落四：恢復與伸展 (Recovery)

**Prompt for Gemini 3 Pro:**
```
Lifestyle sports photography of an athlete foam rolling their back 
on a yoga mat by the riverside at sunset, warm golden hour lighting, 
relaxed but professional atmosphere, high dynamic range, 
sharp focus on the athlete with blurred city background, 
health and wellness magazine aesthetic, 16:9 aspect ratio
```

**用途說明：** 放置於「新手救星：3 招快速恢復法」段落。

---

## 段落五：CTA (RUMA 團隊)

**Prompt for Gemini 3 Pro:**
```
High-energy group photo of a diverse dragon boat team cheering with paddles raised, 
wide angle lens to capture the whole boat, bright vivid colors, 
sun flare reflecting off the water, "Join The Team" vibe, 
modern athletic apparel, capturing genuine joy and camaraderie, 
Sports Illustrated cover quality, 16:9 aspect ratio
```

**用途說明：** 放置於文章結尾 CTA，鼓勵報名體驗。
